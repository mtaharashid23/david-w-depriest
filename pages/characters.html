STEPHEN GODFORD
Commander, First Legion — Humanity's Greatest Military Leader

Once a civilian engineer, Stephen Godford becomes the leader of humanity's first interstellar military force. His strategic brilliance, courage, and commitment to protecting others transform him into one of the most influential figures in human history.

As commander of First Legion, Godford must navigate war, politics, alliances, and personal sacrifice while carrying the hopes of entire worlds. What began as an unlikely path from computer engineering to command led to decisions that would determine the survival of civilizations.

JEFFERY JOHANSSON
Trusted Advisor & Senior Officer

A trusted advisor and loyal officer, Johansson serves as one of Godford's closest allies. His professionalism, leadership, and unwavering support help guide First Legion through some of its greatest challenges. When command falters, Johansson steadies the line.

MERRICK
First Legion Officer

A key figure in the First Legion command structure, Merrick's role in the unfolding conflict proves critical to the fleet's survival. His background and loyalties are among the more complex threads woven through the First Legion saga.
