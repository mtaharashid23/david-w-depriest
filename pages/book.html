Synopsis

FIRST
LEGION

By David W. DePriest

Earth's future changes forever when the Gargans arrive. Bringing advanced technology and a terrifying warning, the alien visitors reveal the existence of a powerful enemy determined to dominate the galaxy.

Humanity responds with unprecedented determination. Massive shipyards are built. Entire fleets are assembled. Millions volunteer. Among them is Stephen Godford, a civilian engineer whose extraordinary leadership abilities place him at the center of Earth's first interstellar military force.

As the newly formed First Legion ventures into the unknown, Godford and his crews discover that not everything is as it seems. Allies hide secrets. Enemies are not what they appear. And the true threat facing the galaxy may be far greater than anyone imagined.
