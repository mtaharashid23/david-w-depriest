THE FIRST LEGION
UNIVERSE

Welcome

A VAST GALAXY AWAITS

The First Legion Universe is a vast science-fiction setting spanning multiple worlds, civilizations, and generations of conflict. It is a universe where humanity has only begun to explore the stars — and where ancient rivalries shape the future of entire galaxies.

Civilizations

ALIEN RACES

FIRST CONTACT SPECIES

THE GARGANS

The Gargans are the first alien civilization to make contact with Earth. Appearing unexpectedly, they bring humanity both advanced technology and a warning of a growing threat.

While they become Earth's earliest allies among the stars, their true motives remain a mystery. Their arrival changes the course of human history forever.

🌐
PRIMARY ANTAGONIST SPECIES

THE TELLNITES

Feared throughout known space, the Tellnites represent one of the greatest military threats humanity has ever encountered. Powerful, relentless, and determined to expand their influence.

They stand at the center of a conflict that spans countless worlds. Their actions force entire civilizations to unite or perish.

⚠
HUMANITY

EARTH'S LEGIONS

Created in response to the growing threat beyond Earth, the Legions represent humanity's first true interstellar military force. Built from the efforts of millions of volunteers and supported by revolutionary technology, the Legions carry humanity's hopes into the unknown.

Organization

MILITARY STRUCTURE

01

The Legions

Earth's primary interstellar military force. Each Legion is a self-contained fighting unit capable of operating independently across vast distances.

02

Fleet Command

The strategic and operational headquarters directing Legion activities across the galaxy, led by Admiral Godford from the Olympia.

03

Ground Forces

Highly trained infantry, engineers, and specialists capable of operating in diverse planetary environments against unknown threats.
