POWERS OF THE GALAXY

01
The First Legion

Earth's first interstellar military fleet — born in desperation, forged in battle. The First Legion represents humanity's best hope against threats it never imagined.

02
The Gargan Empire

A militaristic empire spanning hundreds of star systems. The Gargans have ruled the known galaxy for centuries, viewing humanity as a primitive newcomer that threatens their order.

03
The Tellnite Dominion

Ancient rivals of the Gargans. The Tellnites are scholars, strategists, and political manipulators — always watching, always waiting for the perfect moment to act.

04
Protected Worlds

Neutral or allied planets under the protection of the First Legion. These worlds are humanity's partners in building a galactic coalition against the Gargan threat.

05
Fleet Command

The military and political leadership structure of the First Legion — a complex web of admirals, generals, and council members who must agree on how to fight a war no human has fought before.

06
The Unknown

At the edge of charted space lies something ancient. Something that neither the Gargans nor the Tellnites will speak of. Something that may be the true threat to all intelligent life in the galaxy.

The Conflict

ENEMIES OF HUMANITY

Primary Threat
The Gargans

A militaristic empire that has ruled the known galaxy for centuries. Their technology is centuries ahead of humanity's. Their patience is infinite. Their contempt for human life is absolute.

👁
Secondary Threat
The Tellnites

Ancient rivals of the Gargans and manipulators of galactic politics. Their allegiance shifts like the tide — humanity can never be certain whether the Tellnites are allies, enemies, or something far more dangerous.
