FLEET COMMAND

The Manifest

THE FLEET

The First Legion Fleet represents the largest military undertaking in human history. Designed to protect Earth and its allies, these vessels combine advanced technology, overwhelming firepower, and highly trained personnel.

HSS-001 · FLAGSHIP · COMMAND CARRIER

OLYMPIA

Flagship of First Legion — Admiral Godford's Command

The Olympia serves as the command center for fleet operations and the home of Admiral Stephen Godford. A symbol of humanity's determination, the Olympia leads the fleet into every major engagement. Its advanced command and control systems allow coordinated operations across the entire fleet, while its formidable armaments ensure it can lead from the front when the battle demands it.

Fleet Command · Vanguard Operations · Strategic Command
HSS-002 · HEAVY CRUISER

TIGRESS

Heavy Cruiser — Multi-Role Warship

A versatile warship designed for both fleet combat and independent operations. Known for its durability, firepower, and tactical flexibility, the Tigress is among the most capable ships in the Legion's arsenal.

Fleet Combat · Independent Ops · Escort
HSS-003 · HEAVY CRUISER

FANG

Heavy Cruiser — Front-Line Assault

Built for front-line combat, the Fang serves as one of First Legion's most formidable warships. Its aggressive design and powerful weapons systems make it the spearhead of any major assault.

Assault · Front-Line Combat · Shock Force
HSS-004 · STRIKE CARRIER

VALHALLA

Strike Carrier — Fighter Deployment

The Valhalla carries thousands of fighters and support craft into battle. Its presence often determines the outcome of major engagements, projecting power across vast areas of space.

Air Wing Deployment · Area Denial · Battle Group Anchor
HSS-005 · FLEET CARRIER

MIDAS

Fleet Carrier — Power Projection

Designed to project power across vast distances, the Midas serves as a critical component of First Legion's operational strategy, enabling sustained operations far from support bases.

Power Projection · Sustained Operations · Strategic Reserve
HSS-006 · PERSONNEL CARRIER

TITAN

Personnel Carrier — Troop Transport

The Titan transports the soldiers, specialists, and support personnel who form the backbone of the Legion. Without the Titan and vessels like it, no ground campaign would be possible.

Troop Deployment · Logistics · Ground Force Support
Strategy

TACTICAL DOCTRINE

I

Superior Firepower

Overwhelming force concentration at decisive points. The Legion does not fight fair — it fights to win.

II

Flexible Response

Rapid adaptation to changing battlefield conditions. Every ship in the fleet is capable of independent operations when required.

III

Combined Arms

Seamless coordination between carriers, cruisers, and ground forces ensures maximum combat effectiveness across all domains.
