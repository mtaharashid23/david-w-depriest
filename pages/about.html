The Creator

DAVID W.
DEPRIEST

David W. DePriest is the creator of the First Legion Universe, an expansive military science-fiction franchise exploring humanity's struggle to survive and thrive among the stars.

His stories combine epic space warfare, compelling characters, advanced technologies, and richly detailed worldbuilding to create immersive adventures that capture the imagination of science-fiction readers around the world.

Through First Legion and future installments, David continues to expand a universe filled with courage, exploration, sacrifice, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.
