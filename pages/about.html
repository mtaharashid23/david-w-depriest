The Author

David W.
DePriest

David W. DePriest is the creator of the First Legion Universe — a sweeping military science fiction saga that combines fleet warfare, interstellar politics, alien civilizations, and the indomitable resilience of humanity.

His novels immerse readers in richly detailed worlds where leadership, sacrifice, and courage determine the fate of entire galaxies. The First Legion Universe began with a simple question: what happens when humanity finally reaches the stars — and finds out it isn't welcome?

The answer, DePriest has spent years building, is both terrifying and triumphant. With two books published and more on the way, the First Legion Universe is one of military sci-fi's most ambitious ongoing sagas.
