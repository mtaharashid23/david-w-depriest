Paladin

In 1944, the USS Paladin — a Navy destroyer — disappears during a routine patrol in the Pacific. No wreckage. No survivors. No explanation. The navy classifies her as lost at sea.

Centuries later, in the middle of the First Legion's greatest battle, a 20th century warship materializes from nowhere — weapons hot, crew alive, and carrying intelligence that could change the war. Four ordinary sailors from the past must navigate a universe they never imagined, facing alien civilizations, impossible technology, and a conflict that spans galaxies — all while trying to find a way home.
