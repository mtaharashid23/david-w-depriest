First Legion:
Warlord

Humanity has reached the stars — but the stars are already at war. When Admiral Godford receives orders to take Earth's first interstellar fleet beyond the safety of known space, he quickly discovers the galaxy is not empty, not peaceful, and not welcoming. The Gargan Empire has ruled for centuries. The Tellnite Dominion smolders on the edge of rebellion. And something ancient stirs at the edge of the known universe.

With fractured alliances, impossible odds, and a crew that barely knows the meaning of interstellar warfare, Godford must forge humanity's rawest fleet into an unstoppable force — or watch everything they love burn.
